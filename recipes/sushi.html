Sushi

Description

Sushi is one of the most delicious Japanese dishes conisisting of rice and raw fish.

Ingredients

  • 6 sheets of nori
  • 1 batch sushi rice
  • 1/2 sashimi-grade raw salmon
  • 1 avocado
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Place seaweed on a bamboo mat and cover it with a layer of sushi rice.
  2. Layer the salmon and avocado on the rice.
  3. Roll it up tightly.
  4. Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces with a sharp knife
