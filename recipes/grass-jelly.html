Grass Jelly

Description

Grass jelly is a herbal dessert common in Asian countries. It is created from boiled leaves of mesona plants and rice flour.

Ingredients

  • 100g grass jelly powder
  • 10g cornstarch
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 1000ml water

Steps

  1. Combine the powder, cornstarch, and sugar and add 300ml of water to dissolve
  2. In a saucepan, boil 700ml of water. Slowly stir the mixture into the water. Continue heating the mixture for 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Transfer the mixutre to a heat-safe dish or bowl. Let it rest at room temperature for 30 minutes before transferring to the fridge for at least 2 hours.
